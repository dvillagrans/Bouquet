## Design Context

### Users
Restaurant owners, managers, hosts, waitstaff, kitchen staff, and operators in Mexico who need to coordinate sala, cocina, caja, and payments in real time.

### Brand Personality
Efficient, confident, operational. The brand should feel trustworthy, modern, and clearly built for busy service teams.

### Aesthetic Direction
A premium but practical hospitality SaaS landing page. The current product language leans toward clean editorial minimalism with strong typography, restrained color, and product-forward previews rather than decorative marketing fluff.

### Design Principles
- Make the primary value proposition obvious within seconds.
- Prioritize operational clarity over visual novelty.
- Use typography and spacing to signal hierarchy, not extra decoration.
- Show real product behavior and workflow, not generic SaaS art.
- Keep the tone polished, calm, and credible for restaurant operators.
